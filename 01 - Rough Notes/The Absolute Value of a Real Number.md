### **Definition** 

The absolute value (or modulus) of a real number x (written $| x |$) is a non-negative real number that satisfies the conditions:

## $$
\begin{matrix}
	\begin{align}
		|x| &= x   & \text{if }x \ge 0 \\ 
		|x| &= -x  & \text{if }x < 0
	\end{align}
\end{matrix}
$$
